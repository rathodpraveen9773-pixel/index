Report an Injured Animal

Found an injured, sick or distressed animal? Follow these steps to get help fast.

🐾 What to Do Right Now

Stay calm. Your quick action can save a life. Follow these steps in order.

1

Don't move the animal unless it's in immediate danger

Moving an injured animal incorrectly can worsen internal injuries. Keep people and dogs away to reduce stress on the animal.

2

Identify your location precisely

Note the nearest landmark, building name, and road name. Drop a pin on Google Maps and be ready to share it.

3

Call the right NGO for your area

Use the emergency numbers below or go to the NGO Directory tab to find your area's rescue team.

4

While waiting — provide shade & water if safe

Keep a bowl of water nearby if possible. Don't force-feed or give human medicines. Stay with the animal so rescue team can locate it.

5

Submit a report below so we can track & follow up

Furever Meals tracks reports to connect you with the right help and follow up on the animal's condition.
